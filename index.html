Piskel is a free online editor for animated sprites & pixel art

Don't know where to start? Read about the features of the application by hovering over the yellow squares.

    Use instruments:
  • Pencil
  • Line
  • Flood fill
  • Dropper
  • Eraser

...and 4 pen sizes

    Change and paint in two colors:
  • Left mouse button - Primary color
  • Right mouse button - Secondary color
    Frames capability:
  • Create unlimited quantity
  • Visible ON/OFF
  • Drag'n'Drop
  • Delete
  • Duplicate
    Preview capability:
  • Preview your animation
  • From 0 frames
  • To 24 per second
  • Fullscreen
    Support resolutions:
  • 32x32 px
  • 64x64 px
  • 128x128 px
    Ability to export animations to:
  • Png
  • Gif

Now click on the button to create your own animation!
